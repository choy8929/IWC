HEART OF GOLD

Read Time: 2 min

Meet Jagger Eaton: The accomplished pro skaterboarder, TV personality and IWC brand ambassador shares insights into his journey and drive to make a difference.

  • THE JOY OF SKATEBOARDING

    One moment I’ll never forgot was very recent and it was at the IWC take-over at Jungle Plaza. On the last day of the event, I had the privilege of doing a presentation in front of all the kids of Dibia DREAM. At the end of the presentation they all wanted to learn how to skate so I used my board and helped them all learn the basics.

    Being able to give back to my community has been the most fulfilling act I’ve experienced. Giving skateboards and teaching skateboarding has been my and my family’s objective since the start. I’m so grateful we get the opportunity to help and spread the joy of skateboarding.

  • WHAT IS THE SOURCE OF YOUR BOUNDLESS ENERGY?

    I dedicate each and every day to something I absolutely love to do. I have amazing family, friends and team that support me through thick and thin. I have found my purpose, and for that I will always be grateful.

    WHAT DO YOU AND YOUR WATCH HAVE IN COMMON?

    I’m a huge believer in details and how important they are to everyday life. The Pilot’s Watch Chronograph 41 IW388102 is loaded with amazingly precise details— that to me is what we have in common.

  • WHEN DID YOU DECIDE TO BECOME A PRO SKATEBOARDER?

    When I look back I can’t think of one specific moment nor do I think that was the thought that got me to where I’m at now. My older brother Jett is the reason I’m the skater that I am. We both fell in love with skateboarding at the same time and my whole goal was to keep up with him. He skated the mega ramp with me and got invited to X Games before I ever did. He unfortunately had a bad accident skating mega ramp in 2012 and I made it a goal to make X Games that year in his name. The rest is history.

    WHAT KEEPS YOU FOCUSED BEFORE AND DURING COMPETITIONS?

    Music. I’ve used music as a familiar place to go under high pressure moments ever since I was a kid. I have tons of playlists I use to help me train, compete and relax. It’s been the routine ever since I can remember.
