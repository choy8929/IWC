GISELE’S ART OF GIVING TIME

Read Time: 3 min

Discover how supermodel and powerhouse mom Gisele Bündchen manages her packed schedule and finds “Me Time”

  • THE IMPORTANCE OF SELF-CARE

    The most important thing for me is to start my day right. I feel the way you start sets the energy for your entire day. If you wake up late with the alarm ringing and running late, you feel like you need to catch up for the rest of the day. So, I’m very mindful of the time I wake up.

    Just like you recharge your phone at night, you have to recharge yourself. I’m making sure I have enough sleep. I go to bed at 10 p.m. because I wake up at 5 a.m. I get up before sunrise, and I like to have time to meditate, do some yoga, walk my dog, and prepare breakfast for the kids. I like to do everything in peace, and when everything is calm and quiet. That’s how I fill up my cup, so I am able to give more.

  • GISELE’S NEW BOOK: “NOURISH”

    I have five sisters, so I regularly share recipes with my sisters. I wrote the book because I wanted to share my favorite quick, simple, and yummy recipes with more people because food is such an important thing.

    If you choose the right, healthy food, you’re going to feel better. But a lot of people feel overwhelmed in the kitchen and don’t know what to cook. So, my whole book is about organizing and managing your time because everybody is busy.

    My mom had six kids, so it was all about how she prepped and organized the whole week. If you get organized, you can make sure to get all the produce and nothing goes to waste. My whole book is about nutritious and delicious but simple quick recipes that everyone can make. I am not a chef, but I like to know what I put in my body.
