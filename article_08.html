LIFESTYLE

CAFÉ LEATHER – TIMELESS ESSENTIALS

Read Time: 3 min

For Miguel Sánchez, essentials are key in life. The pillar of everything. Discover how he combines essential IWC timepieces with nearly every look.

  • BACK TO THE BASICS

    In the world of fashion, “back to basics” speaks to the timeless elegance and enduring appeal of simple, well-crafted pieces. It’s about cherishing quality over quantity, embracing versatility and celebrating the beauty of understated sophistication.

    For me, “back to basics” is more than just a style choice - it’s a way of life. It’s about stripping back the distractions and complexities that often clutter our lives, and returning to the core values that ground us and give meaning to our existence.

    “Back to basics” serves as my compass, guiding me towards a life of purpose, passion, and authenticity.

  • ONE WATCH, MANY STYLES

    I’ve always been passionate about the art of curating a wardrobe rooted in well-made essentials and elevated by carefully chosen accessories. Basic/essential clothes are decorated with a special touch through my accessories. A pair of shoes, a nice jacket or my IWC always complete my style. And this is where you understand how versatile an essential a watch can be.

    I’ve been producing the finest leather watch straps for a while, and you cannot imagine how a watch like the Portugieser Chrono can change your look by just changing your strap. From a canvas strap to a leather or stainless steel one, I am able to completely transform the look and feel of my outfit, effortlessly transitioning from casual to formal and from rugged to refined.

  • EVERY DETAIL MATTERS

    Details are the essence of both professional excellence and personal fulfillment. In the world of craftsmanship, it’s the intricate details that elevate a product from good to exceptional. Whether it’s the precision of a hand-stitched leather strap or the sapphire glass that allows you to see the inside of the movement, every detail reflects your commitment to quality and excellence.

    But beyond the professional realm, details hold equal importance in our personal lives or beyond. It’s the small gestures, the thoughtful touches, and the attention to detail that strengthen our relationships and enrich our experiences. Whether it’s remembering a friend’s favorite coffee order or taking the time to notice the beauty in everyday moments, these details demonstrate our care and consideration for others and ourselves, which is also important.
